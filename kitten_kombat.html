Kitten Kombat

  • Overview
  • Indepth rules
  • Cards!

Kitten Kombat is a casual card game with an end goal of being the last cat standing. Take away opposing cats lives with unrealistic weapons such as an Uzi, a Lightsaber, the mighty katana, and a lot more. You aren't completely defenseless against your opponents violent advances on your adorable life, though. Guard yourself with various unconventional armors like the mighty cardboard box, and the cozy blanket.

Nor are you alone in your quest for feline domination. Each player is joined by one or more companion cards. Little critters such as birds, fish, or mice with varying effects from reducing lives lost from hits, to drawing cards from one of the two decks. If you're getting low on life, you can also eat them to regain lives! Even if they're already dead. Hey, kittens aren't picky.



Note: The game is in an early stage of evolution. Things can and likely will change.
